# Specter — Product Hunt Launch

## Listing Details

**Product Name:** Specter

**Tagline:** 7 AI analysts watch every ticker. One verdict. Total vision.

**URL:** https://chippa88.github.io/specter/

**X Account:** @VantaireSystems

**Description:**
Specter is an AI-powered market intelligence terminal that deploys seven specialized analyst agents on any stock, ETF, futures, or crypto ticker. Type a symbol, and within minutes you get a structured briefing with fundamentals analysis, technicals, sentiment, news, bull/bear cases, risk scoring, and a final verdict with confidence rating.

**How it works:**
1. Type any ticker (AAPL, MES, BTC-USD, SPY)
2. Specter deploys all 7 agents simultaneously
3. Receive a structured briefing with verdict and risk assessment

**The 7 Analyst Agents:**
- Fundamentals — Balance sheets, earnings, valuation multiples
- Technical — Chart patterns, support/resistance, momentum
- Sentiment — Fear & greed, put/call ratios, retail sentiment
- News — Real-time headlines, SEC filings, earnings calls
- Bull Case — Upside scenario and catalysts
- Bear Case — Downside risks and headwinds
- Risk Manager — Aggregates all signals into risk score + position sizing

**Pricing:**
- Free: 5 analyses/month, 4 basic agents
- Starter: $29/mo — 50 analyses, all 7 agents, Telegram briefings
- Pro: $59/mo — 200 analyses, Alpaca sync, PDF export
- Elite: $99/mo — Unlimited, broker sync, priority processing

**Launch offer:** First 50 users get Pro for $29/mo (lifetime pricing)
